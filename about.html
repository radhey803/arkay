About us

A Jaipur furniture studio shaped around practical elegance.

We combine custom sizing, functional layouts, and dependable delivery to make every piece feel made for the room it lives in.

Our story

Built to bring warm, durable furniture into Jaipur homes.

Arkay Interior & Furniture started with a simple idea: furniture should fit the home, not force the home to fit the furniture. That approach informs our bedroom sets, dining tables, wardrobes, modular kitchens, and custom joinery.

We work closely with customers to align design, materials, and timing so the final result feels intentional and dependable.

Our timeline
